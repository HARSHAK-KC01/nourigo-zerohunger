Frequently Asked Questions

How can I donate food?

You can donate food by finding a nearby donation point on our interactive map or by contacting us to arrange a pickup for larger donations.

How do I sign up as a volunteer?

You can sign up as a volunteer by filling out the form on our "Get Involved" page. We'll then contact you with opportunities in your area.

Where does the donated food go?

Donated food is distributed to our network of partner organizations, including old age homes, orphanages, and other charitable institutions.
